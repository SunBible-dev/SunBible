Do you know how to get to heaven?

Heaven is and unbelievably wonderful place.
And Hell is an utterly horrible place.
I cannot emphasize enough how important it is for you to spend your entire life trying to get to heaven.
Let me warn you though the way to heaven is straight and narrow, and there can be many who want to lead you astray.
Also never take a man's word for how to get to heaven.
Why?
Because mankind does not know how to get to heaven.
God, the creator of heaven and earth, is the only one that can tell you how to get to heaven.
If I were you I would start a daily habit of reading God's word today.
Why?
Because in His word He tells us how to get to heaven.

To make things easier for you I did compile a list of verses from the Bible that tell us how to get to heaven. But that should not stop you from reading your bible.
