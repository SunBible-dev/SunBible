Hi, my name is N.C.R. I am the founder of The SunShining. A Ministry devoted to providing godly resources. Resources that shine to help people live lives that shine. I have also written over 20 books.
I really like HTML coding, I also really like reading, studying and sharing the Bible. I have seen how other famous authors put together a study bible with their name on it, and I wanted to do the same thing. When I fist started learning HTML coding I was wondering how it would help me. Then I got the idea to make this site.
This site has the entire bible on it. Along with lots of notes and helps by me. I hope to make a study bible in book form someday, but for now I have this site. I am also thinking to use the coding form this site to make phone apps.
This is the home page of The SunShining Bible / NCR Study Bible.
The Bible is the greatest godly resource, and I am pleased to share it with you!
