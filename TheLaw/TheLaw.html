What Laws?
The first 5 books of the Bible are known as the books of the law. And in those books of the law, there are said to be 613 laws made by God. I can't possibly share all those laws on this site, so I encourage you to look them up.
Here is a site I found handy: https://www.gospeloutreach.net/613laws.html
Some of the laws where for that time and place and cannot be practiced by us today. Some of the laws are done away with, like some of the sacrificial ones. Some are just common sense laws. Some I think shouldn't even qualify as laws like the ones that end with "or else you will be unclean". My opinion: "So what if I am unclean". But God is holy and desires us to be holy. So yes it matters if we are clean or not. To God every little detail matters. Which happens to make me mad. (People are weird. They get angry at God for caring. And they get angry at him for not appearing to care.)

Now I want to ask you a question. Does God change? If you are familiar with Malachi 3:6 "For I am the Lord, I change not" and other verses like that one then you know the answer is no. God does not change. His law does not change. Neither does His opinion of the law change. So how can we make sense of these verses that just about say the opposite thing?

One way we can make sense of them is by reading the context. So if you are a serious searcher for truth I recommend you do that. A verse can easily be twisted to say what you want it to say, but if you are looking for truth you will find it.
